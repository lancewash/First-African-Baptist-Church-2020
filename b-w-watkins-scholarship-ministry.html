ELIGIBILITY FOR B.W. WATKINS SCHOLARSHIP

  1. A Candidate must be a member of First African Baptist Church for a minimum of six (6) months and in good standing meeting all of the following criteria:
    • Candidate must have made a financial commitment to the church
    • Candidate attends church as often as possible
    • Candidate participates in the activities of the church
  2. A Candidate must have entered school by the age of twenty-one (21).
  3. A Candidate must be a full-time student and enrolled into a two, four, or combined five-year Bachelors/Masters undergraduate program at an accredited institution of higher learning, carrying at least 12 credits per semester.
  4. A candidate entering other institutions of higher learning (i.e., community or junior college) and will transfer to a four (4) year accredited college, will receive a maximum of eight (8) stipends.
    • A candidate entering a four (4) year program at an accredited college will receive a maximum of eight (8) stipends.
    • A candidate entering other institutions of higher learning (i.e., community or junior college, trade school, business, technical or vocational) must complete all curriculum requirements and receive a maximum of four (4) stipends.
    • A candidate entering a five (5) year program at an accredited college will receive a maximum of ten (10) stipends.
  5. Candidate must complete a B. W. Watkins Scholarship Ministry REQUEST FOR FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE application by the following deadlines: • 3rd Sunday in August for the fall semester • 3rd Sunday in January for the spring semester
  6. Candidate must show proof of admission to his/her selected school by submitting a course schedule. No scholarship will be given without proof of admission.
  7. Candidate must maintain a minimum cumulative 2.0 GPA. No scholarship will be given without a current transcript.
